About Little Minds® Foundation School

Founded with a vision to provide quality education that goes beyond textbooks, Little Minds® Foundation School has been nurturing young minds in Dehradun for over 15 years. Our hands-on approach to education combines traditional values with modern teaching methodologies.

We believe in creating an environment where children feel safe, valued, and encouraged to explore their potential. Our experienced faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and child-centric curriculum ensure that every child receives the attention and guidance they need to thrive.
